About Us

Dreat Music Company is a rising creative powerhouse based in Bangladesh. We focus on nurturing young talents and producing impactful music that resonates worldwide.

Our Artists

Dana - Solo Artist

Dana is a talented Bangladeshi artist studying at Ajou University in South Korea. She rose to fame through university music festivals and national competitions.

MONZO - Boy Group

MONZO blends hip-hop and pop with storytelling and choreography. This five-member group includes four Bangladeshi and one Indian member redefining boy band culture.

Bashi - Instrumental Artist

Bashi fuses traditional Bangladeshi sounds with modern beats. Their album "Beyond Time" brought global attention to South Asian instrumental innovation.

Our Goals

We aim to provide young artists with the support they need to shine. Our goal is to become a global hub for diverse, culturally rich, and innovative music.

Our Location

We are headquartered in Dhaka, Bangladesh, with growing ties to South Korea. We're building a bridge between South Asia and the global music scene.
